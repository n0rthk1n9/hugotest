New: Gluten-Free Crust

Tony’s Pizza

February 15, 2025

We’ve listened to your requests. Starting this week, you can order any pizza on our new gluten-free crust—made in a dedicated prep area to avoid cross-contamination.

Add $3 to any pizza. Available for dine-in, takeout, and delivery.

Please note: While we take every precaution, our kitchen is not a dedicated gluten-free facility. If you have celiac disease or severe sensitivity, please inform your server.
