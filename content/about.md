## Our Story

Tony's Pizza started in 1995 when Antonio "Tony" Rossi brought his grandmother's recipes from Naples to Main Street. What began as a small corner shop has grown into a neighborhood institution—but our commitment to quality has never changed.

## What Makes Us Different

- **Wood-fired oven** — Our 800°F brick oven gives every pizza that perfect char and crisp crust
- **Fresh daily** — Dough made from scratch every morning, never frozen
- **Family recipes** — Secret sauces and techniques passed down through generations
- **Local when possible** — We source from local farms for seasonal specials

## Visit Us

We're more than a restaurant—we're a gathering place. Come for the pizza, stay for the atmosphere. Kids are always welcome, and we're happy to accommodate dietary needs.

*Grazie for being part of our family.*
